Whether you have a fully formed idea or just a rough concept, RAPP makes it easy to turn it into a working application. This guide walks you through the process from start to finish.

## What you'll need

To use RAPP, all you need is a **modern web browser**. Any of these will work:

- Google Chrome
- Mozilla Firefox
- Apple Safari
- Microsoft Edge

That's it. No special software, no downloads, no setup.

<Aside type="tip">
If you're running RAPP locally for development or evaluation, you'll also need Node.js and pnpm. See the [demo setup guide](/rapp-documentation/evaluate/demo/) for those details.
</Aside>

## Creating your first app

Here is the step-by-step process for building an app with RAPP.

<Steps>

1. **Open RAPP**

   Navigate to the RAPP website in your browser. You'll see a landing page with a text field that says something like "Describe the app you want to build."

2. **Describe your app idea**

   Type a short description of what you want to build. For example: "I need a leave management system for my team" or "Build me an expense approval app." You can also click the microphone icon to speak your idea instead of typing.

3. **Choose your path**

   The AI will ask whether you already have a specification document or want to build from scratch.
   - Pick **"I have a spec"** if you have an existing requirements document (PDF, Word, or text file) that describes your app.
   - Pick **"Build from scratch"** if you're starting with just an idea.

4. **Answer the AI's questions**

   If you chose "Build from scratch," the AI will ask you 3 to 5 targeted questions about your app. These are multiple-choice questions designed to fill in the details — things like who will use the app, what the approval workflow looks like, or what notifications you need. Just pick the options that fit your needs.

   If you chose "I have a spec," you'll upload your document and the AI will extract the requirements automatically. No interview needed.

5. **Review your blueprint**

   Once the AI has enough information, it generates a complete blueprint for your app. This includes an overview, user roles, workflows, forms, business rules, and integrations. Take a moment to look it over and make sure it matches what you had in mind.

6. **Open in the builder to refine**

   When you're happy with the blueprint, move to the Builder. This is where you can chat with the AI to make changes, preview your app, and fine-tune every detail.

</Steps>

## What to expect at each step

| Step | What happens | How long it takes |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| Describe your idea | You type or speak a sentence or two | 30 seconds |
| Choose your path | Pick one of two options | A few seconds |
| AI interview | Answer 3-5 multiple-choice questions | 2-3 minutes |
| Blueprint generation | AI creates your app's complete plan | 30-60 seconds |
| Builder | Refine, preview, and manage your app | As long as you need |

<Aside>
You don't need to get everything perfect in the first conversation. The Builder lets you make changes at any time by chatting with the AI.
</Aside>
