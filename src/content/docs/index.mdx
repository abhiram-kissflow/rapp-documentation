## What is RAPP?

RAPP is an AI-powered platform that turns conversations into business applications. Instead of writing code or dragging widgets, you describe what you need — and AI builds it.

- **Talk, don't code** — Describe your app in plain English or speak to it
- **Smart interviews** — AI asks 3-5 targeted questions, infers the rest
- **Complete blueprints** — Get detailed specifications covering users, workflows, forms, and rules
- **Visual builder** — Refine and manage your app through an intuitive editor

## How it works

1. **Describe your idea** — "I need a leave management system"
2. **AI interviews you** — Asks about approval workflows, user roles, and features
3. **Blueprint generated** — Complete specification with processes, forms, and rules
4. **Build and refine** — Edit the blueprint, chat with AI, preview your app
